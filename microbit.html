What is microbit?

The Micro Bit (also referred to as BBC Micro Bit, stylized as Micro:bit) is an open source hardware ARM-based embedded system designed by the BBC for use in computer education in the United Kingdom. It was first announced on the launch of BBC's Make It Digital campaign on 12 March 2015.

What can it do?

The micro:bit can be programmed to do a number of different things, it can be a digital watch, fitness tracker, a robot or a games console.
The device features 25 LED lights and two programmable buttons, which can be used in game-play or to skip through tracks in a playlist.
